Product Info

HozeVoid™ is a revolutionary garden tech company founded by inventor Gary M. in his garage. Its flagship product, the wireless hose, uses proprietary Bluetooth FlowSync™ technology to transport water without a physical pipe—just two smart connectors. Originally dismissed as impossible, the hose went viral after a successful demo, launching HozeVoid into global recognition. Today, HozeVoid offers a full range of futuristic garden tools, from self-weeding robots to app-controlled sprinkler systems. With sleek design, eco-conscious materials, and pure innovation at its core, HozeVoid is redefining how we water the world.
